CINEPR1MO

AN INTERACTIVE VIRTUAL MUSEUM ON FIRST CINEMA EXPERIENCES

Intro

Nowadays, all kinds of expressions, art products and exhibitions are digitized and published on the internet, why not digitize the experiences and transfer them to the future through digital medium? This project was created by Umut Kucuk for the final exam of the Museology, Museography and Virtual Environments (Prof. Simona Caraceni) course.

It is aimed to create a digital museum that contains the records of the first cinema experiences of individuals from different countries and age groups. At this point, recording the cinema experiences of individuals and exhibiting them in a virtual museum is extremely important not only to preserve people's memories or to keep up with the digital, but also to preserve the non-digitized cinema culture.

Theoretical Approach

Concept of the project

Today, digital medium is developing faster than ever before, and the domain of digital medium is increasing excessively. The distribution of motion pictures and their presentation to the audience is gradually being transferred from classical movie theaters to the digital space. Some new motion pictures are now broadcast on digital platforms before movie theaters. In fact, some films are only shown on digital platforms and are never shown in theaters. In addition, with the closing of the movie theaters within the scope of the measures taken due to the pandemic, the films that were already shot to be shown in the movie theaters were presented to the audience also on digital platforms.

There are positive and negative aspects of moving movies from movie theaters to digital platforms, from big screen to small screen (Pardo, 2015).

The positive side of this situation is that movies continue to meet with the audience and cinema workers who make their living from this industry do not lose their jobs.

What is the downside of this situation?

Instead of going to the movies, people watch movies at home on digital platforms. This situation transforms the cinema culture and damages the sustainability of traditional cinema as a whole.

The extreme conformity and rationalization of the culture and habit of watching motion pictures carries the possibility that not only traditional movie theaters cannot find customers, but also reshape the entire cinema industry from the production of films to their content.

This project aims to preserve and sustain the traditional cinema culture with its collection of videos in which individuals from different countries and different age groups describe their first experience of cinema.

How Does It Work?

The visitors uploads the video of their first cinema experience on the website. If the video is in English, it is checked by the editor and published on the website after the necessary arrangements are made. If the language of the video is in a language other than English, the editor contacts the translator who will translate from the relevant language and the translation is performed. The editor adds the text taken from the translator to the video as a subtitle and publishes the video on the website after necessary edits.

Objective and Purpose

The project aims to reach as many individuals as possible and to create a dynamic virtual museum with a wide collection of first cinema experiences with the contributions of people from different countries and different age groups.

With the virtual museum created, it is aimed to draw attention to the traditional cinema culture and to raise awareness in order to preserve this culture.

Museological Approach

Before proceeding further, it is essential to define the concepts of museum and virtual museum. Icom defines the museum: “A museum is a non-profit, permanent institution in the service of society and its development, open to the public, which acquires, conserves, researches, communicates and exhibits the tangible and intangible heritage of humanity and its environment for the purposes of education, study and enjoyment” (ICOM, 2010: 57).

Caraceni, In her thesis named Designing a Taxonomoy for Virtual Museums for the Use of Avicom Professionals, defines virtual museums as follows (Caraceni, 2015: 77):

"With those premises, in my research work, I have defined virtual museums as museums fulfilling the following conditions: 1. they must meet the maximum amount of museological standards defined for tangible museums (Ivarsson, 2004) (ICOM Statutes); 2. application of digital technology (Schweibenz, 1998)."

Technology

"The most important issue here is the creation of a new museum identity using technology."(Caraceni, 2015) Cinepr1mo project differs from other museums with its new and distinctive features and it can be said that it belongs to the new museum identity. Undoubtedly, technology is one of the important structures in the construction of this new identity.

Content

The content of the Cinepr1mo project is formed directly and deeply by the visitor involvement.

Virtual with Real

Although the memories and the experiences of the visitors are abstract, they are completely based on lived events and are real. And these memories are stored digitally and presented virtually.

Visitor Contributions

Visitor contributions are allowed. The essence of the whole project is visitor contributions, which is indispensable for the project, and the museum's collection is enriched with the contributions of visitors.

Pilot Production and Further Developements

The pilot production of the project was carried out completely at no cost. Open source programs such as Aegisub and Shotcut were used in the project, and the project was published on the web via github. In addition to these, ready-made template was used in the creation of the website. No paid server was purchased for videos, google drive was used. Since the project is expected to grow in the future and the number of videos will increase gradually, it is necessary to purchase a cloud server. In addition, more than one translator may be needed for videos that can be recorded in different languages, and more than one editor may be needed depending on the number of video uploads.
